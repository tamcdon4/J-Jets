Pilatus PC-12 (Turboprop)

Seats: up to 8 (+1 pilot)
Range: ~1,500 nm • Cruise: ~250 knots
Luggage: ~40 cu ft

The PC-12 is renowned for reliability and versatility. It offers first-class payload, speed, and range while operating easily in and out of smaller airports.

Cessna Citation Mustang (Light Jet)

Seats: up to 5
Range: ~1,300 nm (≤ 3 pax) • Cruise: 350 knots (400 mph)

The Mustang carries up to five passengers in comfort, offering an efficient and stylish option for short-range business or leisure trips.

Cessna Citation CJ3+ (Light/Midsize Jet)

Seats: up to 8 + 2 pilots
Range: ~2,000 nm • Cruise: ~416 knots

Part of the renowned Citation family, the CJ3+ combines range, reliability, and comfort with fully adjustable executive seating and free Wi-Fi.

Challenger 605 (Interior)

Seats: up to 12 + 2 pilots
Range: > 4,000 nm • Cruise: ~470 knots

The Challenger 605 is our long-range flagship featuring a spacious cabin, luxurious finishes, and global reach with intercontinental comfort.

Challenger 605 (Exterior)

Elegant design meets performance. With a strong airframe and wide-body comfort, the 605 sets the standard for transcontinental luxury travel.
