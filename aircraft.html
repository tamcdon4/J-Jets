Pilatus PC-12 (Turboprop)

Seats: up to 8 (+1 pilot) • Range: ~1,500 nm • Cruise: ~250 knots • Luggage: ~40 cu ft

The PC-12 is renowned for reliability and versatility. It provides first-class payload and short-runway performance with a comfortable, pressurized cabin.

Cessna Citation Mustang (Light Jet)

Seats: up to 5 • Range: up to ~1,300 nm (≤3 pax) • Cruise: 350 knots (400 mph)

A quick, efficient option for business or weekend trips, with a quiet cabin and sleek profile.

Cessna Citation CJ3+ (Light/Midsize Jet)

Seats: up to 8 + 2 pilots • Range: ~2,000 nm • Cruise: ~416 knots

The CJ3+ blends extended range with excellent cabin comfort and executive seating.

HondaJet (Very Light Jet)

Seats: up to 6 • Range: ~1,400 nm • Cruise: ~420 knots

Modern, efficient, and quiet — ideal for regional missions with a bright, roomy cabin.

Challenger 605 (Interior)

Seats: up to 12 + 2 pilots • Range: >4,000 nm • Cruise: ~470 knots

Wide-cabin luxury and intercontinental capability for seamless long-haul comfort.

Challenger 605 (Exterior)
