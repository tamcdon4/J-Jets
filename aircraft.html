Pilatus PC-12 (Turboprop)

  • Seats: up to 8 (+1 pilot)
  • Range: ~1,500 nm • Cruise: ~250 knots
  • Luggage: ~40 cu ft

The Pilatus PC-12 has a reputation for outstanding reliability, comfort, and versatility. It offers first-class payload, speed, and range, operates easily into and out of small airports, and can be quickly reconfigured for passengers and cargo. Equipped with state-of-the-art avionics and powered by a highly reliable turbine engine.

Cessna Citation Mustang (Light Jet)

  • Seats: up to 5
  • Range: up to ~1,300 nm (≤3 pax) • Cruise: 350 knots (400 mph)

The Citation Mustang can carry up to five passengers ~600 nm at 350 knots, and with three passengers or fewer, comfortably reach ~1,300 nm. A stylish, efficient option for quick business trips in comfort and class.

Cessna Citation CJ3+ (Light/Midsize Jet)

  • Seats: up to 8 + 2 pilots
  • Range: ~2,000 nm • Cruise: ~416 knots

Part of the renowned Citation family, the CJ3+ combines reliability and comfort with fully adjustable executive seating and free Wi-Fi. Excellent baggage capacity for skis or golf clubs, plus advanced G3000 synthetic vision for added safety—delivering you in style.

HondaJet (Light Jet)

  • Seats: up to 6
  • Range: ~1,400 nm • Cruise: ~420 knots

A spacious, quiet light jet with best-in-class 66 cu ft cargo capacity. Its Over-The-Wing Engine Mount (OTWEM) reduces drag for performance and efficiency, while higher cruising altitudes often mean smoother flights. A favorite for comfort, speed, and fuel efficiency.

Bombardier Challenger 605 (Heavy Jet)

  • Seats: up to 12
  • Range: ~4,000 nm • Cruise: 470 knots

The Bombardier Challenger 605 is a luxurious business jet known for its comfort and performance. It seats 12 passengers in a spacious cabin, offering a smooth, quiet ride with plenty of room to move around. With a range of 4,000 nautical miles, it can fly non-stop between major cities like New York to London or Los Angeles to Hawaii. Powered by efficient General Electric engines, it cruises at 470 knots, ensuring a fast and safe journey.
